MEDICAL FACILITIES

With the growth of Kurukshetra and emergence of tourism here, medical facilities have also improved in this city manifold. Today, Kurukshetra has some of the best and most advanced hospitals that offer complete treatment of all diseases. There are many government level hospitals including the Civil Hospital of Kurukshetra and other health care centers. Private multi specialty hospitals are also established in this city to offer advanced treatment in cases of complex illnesses. These hospitals in Kurukshetra are run by efficient medical staff with better infrastructure for the care of patients.
